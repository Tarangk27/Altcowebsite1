Our Initiatives

At ALT CO, we believe in creating beautiful products that make a positive impact. Our commitment extends beyond aesthetics to ethical practices and environmental responsibility.

Artisan Support

We honor traditional craftsmanship by paying artisans 30% above market rates. Each purchase directly supports skilled craftspeople and their communities, preserving cultural heritage while providing sustainable livelihoods.

Sustainable Sourcing

Our products are crafted from responsibly sourced materials. We use reclaimed wood, recycled metals, and organic textiles wherever possible. Every item is designed to minimize environmental impact while maximizing longevity.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

We've eliminated plastic from our packaging, using only biodegradable materials. Our boxes are made from recycled paper, and we use vegetable-based inks and natural fillers to ensure every aspect of our product journey is earth-friendly.

Minimizing Carbon Footprint

We are actively reducing our carbon footprint by adopting green energy sources for production and minimizing transportation emissions. Our goal is to achieve net-zero emissions by 2030.

About Us

ALT CO was founded with a simple mission: to create everyday essentials that combine beauty, functionality, and ethical production.

We believe that the objects we surround ourselves with should not only look good but also feel good in their impact on the world. Each product in our collection is thoughtfully designed to complement your individual style while being consciously crafted.

Our team works directly with artisans across the globe, building lasting relationships and ensuring fair compensation for their remarkable skills. We're committed to creating a more sustainable and equitable approach to home goods.
